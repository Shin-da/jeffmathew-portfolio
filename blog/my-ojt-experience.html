My On-the-Job Training Experience at SP Madrid & Associates Law Firm

2025-05-20

Introduction

I recently completed my 500-hour On-the-Job Training (OJT) as a Developer and AI Prompt Engineer at SP Madrid & Associates Law Firm, an experience that significantly enhanced my skills in web development, AI, and automation. This post will delve into my responsibilities, key achievements, and the valuable lessons I learned during my internship.

Our OJT team at SP Madrid & Associates Law Firm

About SP Madrid & Associates Law Firm

SP Madrid & Associates is a forward-thinking law firm that embraces technology to enhance their legal services. Located in a modern office environment, the firm specializes in various legal areas while leveraging AI and automation tools to streamline their operations. What impressed me most was their commitment to innovation and their willingness to integrate cutting-edge technology into traditional legal practices.

SP Madrid & Associates Law Firm

My Role and Responsibilities

As an intern, my primary responsibilities included:

  • Enhancing and maintaining AI agents: I worked on improving the efficiency and multi-interaction handling capabilities of AI systems, particularly focusing on client interaction bots and document processing automation.
  • Developing automation solutions: I utilized Python and various APIs to create scripts and tools that streamlined internal workflows, including document generation, data extraction, and report automation.
  • Creating and optimizing AI prompts: A significant part of my role involved crafting effective prompts for AI operations and client services, ensuring accurate and helpful responses.
  • Team Collaboration: I actively collaborated with the team to implement new features and improve overall user experience, participating in regular meetings and code reviews.

Working on AI development and automation projects

Key Achievements

During my OJT, I am proud to have:

  • Successfully optimized several AI agents, leading to smoother and more efficient user interactions with a 40% improvement in response accuracy.
  • Developed Python-based automation scripts that reduced manual workload by 60% and improved data processing efficiency.
  • Contributed to the creation of new AI prompts that enhanced the accuracy and relevance of AI responses for legal document analysis.
  • Gained hands-on experience in a professional development environment, applying theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges.
  • Created a comprehensive OJT tracking system that helped monitor progress and achievements throughout the internship.

OJT Tracker System I developed to monitor progress

Skills Gained

This OJT experience was instrumental in developing and solidifying a range of technical and soft skills, including:

  • Technical Skills: Python programming, AI Development, API Integration, Workflow Automation, Web Development Fundamentals, Database Management, Version Control (Git).
  • Soft Skills: Problem-solving, Critical Thinking, Team Collaboration, Communication, Adaptability, Time Management, Professional Ethics.

Learning AI development techniques

Collaborating with the development team

Challenges and Learnings

One of the biggest challenges I faced was adapting to the fast-paced environment of a professional law firm while learning new technologies. Initially, I struggled with understanding the legal terminology and requirements, but through persistence and guidance from my mentors, I learned to bridge the gap between technical implementation and legal needs.

I also learned the importance of documentation and clear communication. Working with AI systems required precise prompt engineering, and I discovered that small changes in wording could significantly impact the quality of AI responses. This taught me to be more methodical and detail-oriented in my work.

Problem-solving sessions with the team

Working on AI interface development

Memorable Moments

One of my most memorable experiences was when I successfully implemented an AI-powered document analysis system that could automatically extract key information from legal documents. Seeing the system work efficiently and receiving positive feedback from the legal team was incredibly rewarding. It was a moment that validated my choice to pursue a career in technology.

Another highlight was participating in team meetings where I could contribute ideas and see them being implemented. The collaborative environment taught me the value of diverse perspectives and how different viewpoints can lead to better solutions.

AI interface implementation for document analysis

Conclusion

My OJT at SP Madrid & Associates Law Firm was an invaluable experience that exceeded my expectations. It provided me with practical exposure to AI development and automation in a legal technology setting, while also teaching me about professional work ethics and team dynamics.

I am grateful for the opportunity to contribute to meaningful projects and learn from experienced professionals who were always willing to share their knowledge. This internship has strengthened my resolve to pursue a career at the intersection of technology and creativity, and I'm excited to apply these skills in future opportunities.

The experience has also given me a deeper appreciation for how technology can enhance traditional industries like law, and I'm motivated to continue learning and growing in this field.
