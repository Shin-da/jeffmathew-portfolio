My Story

Hi, I’m Jeff! I’m passionate about blending technology and creativity to build things that make people’s lives better and more beautiful. Whether I’m coding a web app, designing a user interface, or sketching a new piece of art, I believe in the power of curiosity and imagination.

When I’m not coding or drawing, you’ll probably find me watching anime, reading manga, or vibing to K-pop and OPM.

My journey has taken me from drawing in sketchbooks to developing full-stack applications and exploring the frontiers of AI. I love learning new things, solving real-world problems, and collaborating with others who share a drive for innovation.

My philosophy? “Art and code are two sides of the same creative coin.” I strive to bring empathy, clarity, and a touch of artistry to every project I work on. Let’s create something amazing together!
